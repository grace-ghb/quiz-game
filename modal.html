Quiz Game

In this quiz game, you can choose to play the Easy version where you have to match the country with their capital city. You can choose the answer from choice of A, B or C. To play this version, click the 'Easy' button.

If you are ready to challenge yourself, you can choose the DIFFICULT version. In this version you have to key in your own word. To play this version, click the 'Difficult' button.
